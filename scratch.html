How to Whip

  1. Set up the Approach
    • Choose the right jump
    • Line up your bike
  2. Initiate the Whip Mid-Flight
    • Turn the bars and lean slightly
    • Use throttle and body position to turn the whip
  3. Bring the Bike Back for Landing
    • Counter-lean to straighten out
    • Spot your landing and absorb the impact
